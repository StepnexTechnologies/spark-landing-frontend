We are the **intelligence infrastructure for the $300B Creator Economy**. We deploy an AI Agent Team — powered by our proprietary Creator Genome — that handles the strategic, financial, and legal rails so creators can stay creative while their business thrives.

Most creators have the influence of a large company but lack the tools to run one. They spend too much time on messy admin work and spreadsheets. We replace that manual chaos with purpose-built AI.
